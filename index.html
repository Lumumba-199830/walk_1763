Tennis is a fast-paced sport played between two players, singles, or two teams of two, doubles. The goal is to hit the ball over a net and land it within the opponents court. It requires a mix of physical fitness, quick reflexes and strategic thinking, as players use different shots like serves, volleys and groundstokes to outplay each other. Played on surfaces such as grass, clay or hard courts, tennis is enjoyed worldwide both recreationally and professionally.
